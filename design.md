# Design System - Sadhana Yogshala

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Background**: `#FFFFFF` (White)
- **Primary**: `#31694e` (Dark green - main brand color)
- **Secondary**: `#658c58` (Medium green)
- **Accent**: `#bbc863` (Olive green)
- **Foreground**: `#31694e` (Dark green for text)

### Additional Palette Colors
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` (Card backgrounds, text on dark buttons)

### Functional Colors
- **Text Primary**: `#31694e` (Dark green)
- **Text Secondary**: `#658c58` (Medium green for muted text)
- **Border/Accent**: `#bbc863` (Olive green for borders and accents)

## Typography

### Fonts
- **Headings**: `Playfair Display` (Serif, elegant, matches the "YOGA FIRST" style)
- **Body**: `Inter` or `Lato` (Clean sans-serif)

### Scale
- **H1 (Hero)**: Bold, Uppercase, ~64px
- **H2 (Section Titles)**: Bold, ~48px
- **H3 (Card Titles)**: Semi-bold, ~24px
- **Body**: Regular, 16px

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**:
    - Background: Dark Green (`#31694e`)
    - Text: White
    - Radius: Rounded (approx 8px-12px)
    - Padding: px-6 py-3
- **Secondary**:
    - Background: Transparent
    - Text: Dark Green (`#31694e`)
    - Icon: Arrow circle right

### Cards
- **Service Card (Default)**:
    - Background: Light olive green with opacity (`#bbc863` at 30% opacity)
    - Border: None (Shadow/Elevation)
    - Radius: ~20px
    - Content: Icon, Title, Text, "Learn More" button (outlined)
    - Hover: Transforms to highlighted state (dark green background, white text)
- **Service Card (Active/Highlighted)**:
    - Background: Dark Green (`#31694e`)
    - Text: White
    - Button: White background, Dark green text

### Layout
- **Container**: Centered, max-width ~1200px
- **Spacing**: Generous whitespace (80px-100px between sections)

## Sections (Based on Screenshots)

1.  **Header**: Transparent background, overlaying the Hero. White text/logo when on Hero.
2.  **Hero**: Full-screen background image (Mountain landscape, nature, meditation). Centered text, White typography.
3.  **Services**: "Our Services" centered heading. Horizontal list of cards.
4.  **Latest Classes**: "Latest Classes" heading. Tab navigation. Split layout (Image Left, Content Right with numbered circular steps).
5.  **Membership**: Parallax/Image background. Floating white card on right with "Free 30-Day Membership" offer.
6.  **Footer**: Minimalist. Logo, Social Icons, Copyright.
